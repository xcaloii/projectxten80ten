About Ten80Ten

We got tired of watching good people do robot work.

So we built a different kind of agency — one that ships the software and supplies the operator, then owns the outcome. Capability over hourly rate. Results over timesheets.

Our belief

The work shouldn't depend on someone remembering.

Every business has a layer of rule-based work that repeats the same way forever: the follow-up, the intake, the reminder, the hand-off. It's predictable — which is exactly why a person shouldn't have to hold it in their head.

Traditional VA agencies rent you another set of hands and leave the broken process untouched. We do the opposite: fix the process, build the system, then put a trained specialist on top. The knowledge lives in the system — so it never walks out the door.

"Empower your core, delegate the chore."

The principle behind every engagement we take.

Systems own the repeatable work
Specialists own the judgement calls
You own your time back
The 10/80/10 framework

The math behind the name.

Ten percent of the effort, eighty percent automated, ten percent of ongoing attention. It's how a recurring chore becomes a system you never think about again.

10%Setup

We map your repetitive work and build the system to run it. You see it handle your exact task before committing a dollar.

80%Delegate

System and specialist carry the daily load automatically — quietly, reliably, without you in the loop.

10%Review

Monthly monitoring, reporting and optimization. We tune the system as your business changes.
